 Domanda: trentatre trentini entrarono a trentro tutti e trentatre trotterellando 
 Risposta: A classic Italian phrase!

The literal translation is: "Thirty-three Trentines entered Trento all thirty-three trotting."

But the idiomatic meaning is: "All 33 of them went to Trento in a single file, one after another." The verb "trotterellando" emphasizes their slow and deliberate pace.

This phrase is often used humorously or ironically to describe someone (or something) that's moving very slowly.
